## Price Prediction Model

### Factors Affecting Case Price
1. **Age** (strongest factor): Older = Higher value (+exponential)
2. **Supply**: Fewer listings = Higher price
3. **Demand**: Collector interest, seasonal spikes
4. **Rarity**: Operation cases > Major cases
5. **Market Sentiment**: CS2 popularity, new updates
6. **Seasonality**: +20-30% spike Nov-Dec

### Simple Prediction Formula
```
Future Price = Current Price × (1 + Annual Growth Rate)^Years

Where Annual Growth Rate = Age Factor
- 0-1 years old: -15% to +10%

- 4-8 years old: +20% to +30%

```
